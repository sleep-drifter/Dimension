KS

Katrin Schacke is an independent graphic designer, art director, illustrator and photographer based in Offenbach am Main, Germany. She works in many interdisciplinary fields of design, including editorial design, book and catalog design, conception and visualization of images by the use of illustration, installation and photography, as well as exhibition design, poster design, identities and web design.
