U

For more than ten years the European design collective Underware has been creating versatile fonts, conducting type workshops, and running the radio station Typeradio. Their work has been highly praised by designers and typographers throughout the world. Underware runs an amazing type blog which features their work and other topics revolving around traditional and experimental typography.

Underware was founded in 1999 by Akiem Helmling, Bas Jacobs and Sami Kortemäki and is based in Den Haag, Helsinki, and Amsterdam.
